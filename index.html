How this board works

Create, edit, delete, and move tasks across categories. Tasks can include details, a start date, and a due date.

Tasks can be reordered inside a category and dragged to another category with a long press. Categories can also be reordered by long-press dragging their heading on desktop or their tab on mobile.

On mobile, drag a task onto a category tab to move it there, then keep dragging within the visible list to fine-tune its position.

Use the category dialog to rename a category, choose a preset background color, and control whether due date progress is shown for tasks in that category.

Use board settings to rename the board, export or import data, reset the board to its defaults after confirmation, or reset category colors.

On desktop, categories are shown in columns. On mobile, categories are shown as tabs.

All board data and settings are stored locally in IndexedDB in this browser.
